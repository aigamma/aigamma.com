Live market dashboards. The homepage at aigamma.com hosts the gamma inflection chart, the gamma map, the SPX vs vol flip chart, the gamma regime history chart, the gamma index oscillator, and the gamma index versus realized volatility scatter. These are the live dealer-positioning dashboards that anchor the rest of the platform.

Tactical volatility surface. The tactical page at aigamma.com/tactical hosts the volatility risk premium chart with Yang-Zhang realized vol context, the term structure chart with historical distribution clouds across listed expirations, the Breeden-Litzenberger risk-neutral density extraction across nine expirations, and the day-over-day fixed-strike implied volatility matrix. The multi-model volatility smile model (Heston, Merton, SVI raw concurrent fits with reader-toggle visibility) was migrated off this page on 2026-05-06 to anchor the top of the /stochastic/ lab above the Hagan SABR model.

VIX analytics. The VIX page at aigamma.com/vix hosts the Cboe vol term structure across six points, a contango history chart, a VRP-for-VIX comparison, an Ornstein-Uhlenbeck calibration on log-VIX with 60-day forward projection, the VVIX-versus-realized-vol-of-VIX second-order VRP, the VVIX/VIX ratio vol-of-vol complacency indicator with amber and coral threshold bands at 5 and 6 flagging when VIX is suppressed relative to VVIX, a cross-asset vol comparison framework, the Nations SkewDex (SDEX) versus TailDex (TDEX) skew-and-tail-cost chart that decomposes the SPY tail premium into normalized smile slope versus running 30 DTE 3-sigma put price, a discrete VIX regime classifier with Markov transition matrices, and the Cboe strategy benchmark suite covering BXM, BXMD, BFLY, and CNDR.

Seasonality and rotation. The seasonality page at aigamma.com/seasonality hosts the intraday SPX cumulative-move matrix across 30-minute windows with multi-window aggregations from 5 to 40 trading days. The stocks page at aigamma.com/stocks hosts horizontal-bar performance rankings of eleven curated high-options-volume single names across 1-day, 5-day, and 21-day horizons, plus the Roy Mansfield-normalized relative stock rotation plane covering twenty single names against SPY.

Sector and stock screening. The heatmap page at aigamma.com/heatmap hosts the equal-tile top-250-by-options-volume sector heatmap organized into eleven GICS sector bands, with same-day percent-change coloring. This is a deliberate departure from the conventional market-cap-weighted treemap.

Events and earnings calendars. The earnings page at aigamma.com/earnings hosts the upcoming-earnings scatter for the next five trading days filtered by options volume tier, with implied-range computations from the ATM straddle and a four-week calendar grid. The events page at aigamma.com/events hosts the four-week macro events calendar with implied-move overlays at the next listed expiration, a hero countdown panel, sticky pin behavior, a timeline strip, a per-family spotlight strip, and a chronological day schedule with forecast interpretation and earnings-layer toggle.

Expiring gamma diagnostics. The expiring-gamma page at aigamma.com/expiring-gamma hosts the per-expiration dealer dollar-gamma roll-off chart with calls and puts mirrored, frozen-book unwind framing, and the standard dealer-hedging unit of dollars per one percent move.

Discrete pricing and parametric smile fits. The discrete page at aigamma.com/discrete hosts CRR binomial trees with European and American exercise, Boyle-Kamrad-Ritchken trinomial trees with stretch parameter lambda equals root-three, and convergence diagnostics against the Black-Scholes reference. Also hosts SVI in raw, natural, and Jump-Wings parameterizations with Levenberg-Marquardt calibration and Durrleman g(k) butterfly arbitrage diagnostics, plus SSVI joint surface fitting with calendar-arbitrage guarantees and per-tenor RMSE comparison against raw SVI.

GARCH ensemble. The garch page at aigamma.com/garch hosts a 17-specification GARCH ensemble across ten model families covering symmetric, asymmetric, power, absolute, component, in-mean, score-driven, long-memory, regime-switching, and realized variants, with conditional sigma paths and 30-day forward forecasting.

Stochastic volatility and jump models. The jump page at aigamma.com/jump hosts Merton with log-normal jumps, Kou with asymmetric double-exponential jumps, Bates SVJ with eight-parameter calibration combining Heston variance and Merton jumps, and Variance Gamma as a pure-jump infinite-activity Levy process. The smile page at aigamma.com/smile hosts the multi-model Volatility Smile as the single reading surface on the page: Heston with CIR variance, Merton diffusion-plus-jumps, and SVI raw concurrent fits on one expiration slice with reader-toggle visibility (Heston enabled by default). The /stochastic/ URL 301-redirects to /smile/ for any reader still using a bookmarked link.

Local volatility. The local page at aigamma.com/local hosts Dupire extraction with Monte Carlo pricing self-check using antithetic sampling, smile and term-structure slice navigation across the surface grid, the Gyongy-projection forward-smile flattening diagnostic motivating LSV augmentation, and the whole-surface Dupire local-volatility heatmap in (log-moneyness, T) coordinates as the closing model.

Regime detection. The regime page at aigamma.com/regime hosts the 2-component EM Gaussian mixture lognormal model, the Hamilton 2-state Markov-switching model with regime-conditional transition matrices, and Wasserstein K-means clustering on rolling 20-day windows with three regime buckets.

Hedge-ratio analysis. The risk page at aigamma.com/risk hosts Vanna-Volga three-anchor smile reconstruction with risk-reversal and butterfly decomposition, cross-model Greeks comparing BSM, Bachelier, and Heston, the four-way delta comparison covering BSM, sticky-delta, Hull-White minimum-variance, and market deltas, and second-order Greeks (vanna, volga, charm) across the strike ladder.

Rough volatility. The rough page at aigamma.com/rough hosts the Bayer-Friz-Gatheral rough Bergomi simulator with four-parameter regime control over Hurst, vol-of-vol, correlation, and initial vol, the rough Bergomi skew term-structure scaling-law fit that reads H off today's SVI surface in closed form via the analytic SVI tangent at y equals zero, the Gatheral-Jaisson-Rosenbaum RFSV structure function for Hurst estimation across q values, and the three-estimator Hurst triangulation across variogram, absolute moments, and DFA with spread-based reliability scoring.
